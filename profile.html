A Brief About Me

Hello

Davide Falanga was born near Naples in 1989. He received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Automation Engineering from University of Naples "Federico II" in 2012 and 2015, respectively.

He is currently a PhD student at the Robotics and Perception Group, University of Zurich, under the supervision of Prof. Davide Scaramuzza. The group's mission is to develop autonomous flying vehicles that can navigate in any environment using only onboard cameras.
